Hi! I'm Giancarlo

A frontend developer living in Buenos Aires, who is passionate about JavaScript ecosystem, enjoys coding and birdwatching.

Who I Am

I have a bachelor degree in Biology with mention in Zoology, from San Marcos National University in Peru.

After a few years studying ecology of some species of birds, mainly parrots and raptors from the Coast and Amazon rainforest of Peru, I moved to Argentina where I found my other passion in life: coding.

I worked as a freelancer since the early years of web development, coding web templates for different clients with just HTML/CSS. Over time I added JavaScript to my skill set, expanding my chances of being able to work on more complex projects.

Always in constant training, that is why I am increasing my skills in React, Node and GraphQl, technologies that will allow me to create robust applications that solve real people needs.
One of my goals is to keep going in coding for good.

Father of a lovely and smart daughter of five years old, I spend part of my free time watching nature tv shows and cartoons with her.
